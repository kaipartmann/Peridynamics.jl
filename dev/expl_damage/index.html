Damage in peridynamics formulations

Bond-based formulation

\[ \boldsymbol{f} = d^{ij} \, c \, \varepsilon^{ij} \, \boldsymbol{n} \; .\]

with

sizesymbolunit
bond failure$d^{ij} \in \{0;1\}$$[-]$

In this expression, bond failure $d^{ij}$ represents whether the bond between points $i$ and $j$ is intact ($d^{ij}=1$) or damaged ($d^{ij}=0$).

with the parameters:

sizesymbolunit
